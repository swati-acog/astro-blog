Summer 2026 has been one for the books. I decided to swap my dual-monitor setup for the pristine beaches of the Mediterranean, and it was the best decision I've made all year.

### Disconnecting to Reconnect
We often feel the pressure to be 'always on'. Spending a week without Slack notifications felt like a superpower. Instead of scrolling through feeds, I was watching the sunset over the Amalfi Coast.

### The Travel Hack: Slow Travel
Instead of rushing through five cities in ten days, I spent the entire time in one small village. This allowed me to find the best local espresso, make friends with the baker, and actually feel rested.

### A Bit of Inspiration
Interestingly, being away from my usual environment gave me several new ideas for my open-source projects. Sometimes the best way to solve a bug is to stop looking at the screen and start looking at the horizon.
