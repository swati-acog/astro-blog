If you've been following my work, you know I'm a bit of a performance nerd. When Astro 6 dropped recently, I knew I had to migrate this portfolio immediately. Here's why.

### Zero JS by Default
The islands architecture is still the king of web performance. Shipping zero kilobytes of JavaScript to the client for content-heavy pages is a breath of fresh air in an era of bloated frameworks.

### Server Actions and Enhancements
Astro 6 has made handling forms and server-side logic so much more intuitive. The API feels cleaner, and the type safety is rock solid.

### The Community
The ecosystem around Astro is growing incredibly fast. From integrations to themes, there's always something new to explore.

I'm currently working on a custom integration for our internal tools—stay tuned for a deep dive into that next week!
