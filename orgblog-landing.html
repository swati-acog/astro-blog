What OrgBlog does

Everything your team needs to share and grow

Collaborative blogging
Multiple authors can co-publish a single post. Every contributor is credited and visible on the card and the full article.
Instant search
Find any post by title, author name, or topic tag — instantly. No page loads, no waiting.
Employee directory
Contact details auto-extracted from resumes by the backend pipeline. Always up to date, zero manual entry.
Topic filtering
Posts are tagged by department — Engineering, Design, Culture, Data, and more. Filter to what matters to you.
Minimal by design
No clutter, no noise. A clean reading experience that keeps the focus on the writing and the people behind it.
Backend-ready
Built to plug into your existing HR and auth systems. The directory and post data are easily wired to real APIs.
